My Hobbies

Bodybuilding

Going to the gym is a major part of my life. I always try to pull at least 3 days a week, minimum, of hitting the gym. Bodybuilding has helped me release stress from my studies, and simply keep my mind off things. I enjoy seeing my physique improve, hard work pays off. In the future, I hope to compete in a natural BodyBuilding competition.

Marathons

Similar to the topic of exercise, I enjoy running just as much as Bodybuilding. However, Marathons in particular, has been an activity I always take part in. The thrill of having hundreds of people running with you, the excitement and energy, is something I enjoy. My last marathon was in Abu Dhabi, where I covered 10km in about 1 hour.

Video Games

Computer games will be a part of my life as long as Steam exists. If I am not in the gym, or studying, and I feel that I deserve a break, I open up my computer and play some Fallout 4, or Terraria, or some other classic favorites.
